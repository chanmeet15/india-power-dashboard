Goa Power Sector — Key Facts
📋 Administration
Utility: Goa Electricity Dept (GED) / GEDCL
Transmission: GETCO / PGCIL (central)
SLDC: Goa SLDC (goaelectricity.gov.in)
Regional grid: Western Region (WRLDC)
⚡ Supply Profile
Peak demand: ~600–680 MW (summer)
Annual energy: ~4,200–4,600 MU
RE installed: ~95+ MW (solar dominant)
Storage: Planned BESS under RDSS
T&D losses: ~12–15%
🔌 Central Sector
Main supplier: NTPC (Mouda, Korba, Sipat)
Nuclear: NPCIL Tarapur allocation
Hydro: Sardar Sarovar, Koyna
Exchange: IEX DAM/RTM + bilateral
Electricity Derivatives — NSE & MCX Futures
ℹ️ About NSE & MCX Electricity Futures
NSE ELECMBL — Monthly Electricity Futures launched 14 July 2025. Cash-settled against IEX Day-Ahead Market VWAP. Unit: 50,000 kWh per lot. Quoted in ₹/MWh.
MCX ELEC — MCX Electricity Futures launched 10 July 2025. Settled against IEX DAM UMCP. Unit: 50 MWh per lot. Tick size: ₹1/MWh.
Both contracts trade from 9:00 AM to 11:30 PM IST. SEBI-approved. Zero exchange fees on NSE until Dec 2025.
